I encourage students to develop their AI literacy skills during this course by using AI tools in ways that comply with the University's and this course's academic honesty policy. The University of Georgia provides students free access to several AI resources that meet its privacy and data security standards, including \href{https://uga.teamdynamix.com/TDClient/3190/eitsclientportal/KB/Article/161202/Microsoft-Copilot-with-Commercial-Data-Protection}{Microsoft Copilot}, \href{https://uga.teamdynamix.com/TDClient/3190/eitsclientportal/KB/Article/167237/Gemini-with-Customer-Data-Protection}{Google Gemini}, and \href{https://uga.teamdynamix.com/TDClient/3190/eitsclientportal/KB/Article/167126/NotebookLM-with-Customer-Data-Protection}{Google's NotebookLM}. Other AI tools, like OpenAI's ChatGPT and Anthropic's Claude, offer free tiers of their services. See \href{https://aihub.uga.edu/resources-tools/students/}{https://aihub.uga.edu/resources-tools/students/} for more on AI resources available to UGA students.
