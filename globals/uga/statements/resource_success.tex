The Office for Student Success and Achievement (OSSA) empowers students to achieve success throughout their academic journey. Through free peer tutoring, academic coaching, UNIV student success courses, Bulldog Basics, and first-generation student support, we promote well-being, student learning, and community building. To connect with OSSA, email \texttt{ossa@uga.edu}, call (706) 542-0163, or visit Milledge Hall (near Reed Hall and Sanford Stadium).
